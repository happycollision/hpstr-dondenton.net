A Very Denton Christmas

Recorded for your listening pleasure in Fall 2011

Don and Daniel thank you for stopping by to listen to the album. This project was originally a light-hearted gift for their family, recorded when they were together visiting their parents for Christmas in December 2011. Recorded and mastered by Dan. Merry Christmas!
